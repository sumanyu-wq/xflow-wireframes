Common questions

FAQ

Is Xflow cheaper than Skydo for large invoices?
+
Xflow charges 0.6% flat (min $12) on any invoice size. For a $10K invoice, Xflow costs $60 vs Skydo's $29 — but Skydo doesn't include free FIRA (Wise charges $2.50 per FIRA), and Wise charges $172 for the same $10K invoice. At $25K+, Wise's $10K cap makes it unusable entirely. Xflow has no limits.
Why can't I use Wise for my Pvt Ltd company?
+
Wise's Indian business accounts only support freelancers and sole proprietors — registered entities like Pvt Ltd, LLP, or OPC are not supported. Xflow supports all Indian business structures.
What is an EEFC account and does Xflow support it?
+
An EEFC (Exchange Earners' Foreign Currency) account lets you hold foreign currency and convert when rates are most favourable. Xflow supports EEFC natively. Neither Skydo nor Wise offer this — you're forced to convert immediately at whatever rate is available on receipt.
How does Xflow handle FIRA / eFIRC compliance?
+
Xflow auto-generates eFIRA within 24 hours of receipt — always free. Skydo also provides FIRA free. Wise charges $2.50 per FIRA, which adds up significantly for frequent exporters.
Is Xflow safe and RBI regulated?
+
Yes. Xflow is an RBI-authorised payment aggregator using JP Morgan as its banking infrastructure partner. The platform is ISO 27001 + SOC 2 certified with no limits on transfer size.
